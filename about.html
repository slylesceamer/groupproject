About Eats RSVP

Eats on Reserve is a startup non-profit organization driven to provide uneaten and unused leftover food to benefit the less fortunate in need of food. We aim in assisting the charitable communities of all sizes, whether it's the National Red Cross Society, the religious sectors via soup kitchens and public school free meal programs. Our goal is purely simple in aiding the ability of anyone to get a nice meal to consume. A good meal goes a long way to a great day. This is our mission statement and our creed "Everyone's gotta eat so why not you".

Eats on reserve no bias, no issues, and no problems. You submit a request via your contact info and email. We inform you what food is leftover aka "eats on reserve" and then the person, family or organizations decides whether they would accept the food or not. Although food type and portions are limited based on what's provided to us by the charitable sources and restaurants we are associated with. Eats on reserve tries it's very best to get the most diverse and healthy selections in its reserves for variety purposes. We aren't perfect but we strive diligently to do good in the community by filling up as many starved stomachs as physically possible.
